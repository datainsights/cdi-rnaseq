Q&A 1 Welcome to CDI – From Reads to Insights in RNA-Seq

1.1 This guide helps you explore RNA-Seq datasets from count matrices to quality control. You’ll gain confidence in examining expression patterns and preparing gene-level summaries for downstream use.

1.2 📚 The CDI Learning Path

This guide is part of the CDI layered learning system, where insights from complex data are explored step by step through four progressive layers:

  • 🔍 EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis) – Start here. Understand your data, its structure, and quirks.
  • 📊 VIZ (Visualization) – Learn how to communicate patterns and insights visually.
  • 📐 STATS (Statistical Analysis) – Apply statistical methods to test hypotheses and quantify uncertainty.
  • 🤖 ML (Machine Learning) – Use models to predict, classify, and extract deeper meaning.

You’re currently reading the Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) layer — the free, open-access starting point of the CDI layered learning system.

Whether you’re preparing for a remote role, analyzing real-world data, or mentoring others, CDI helps you grow — one Q&A at a time.
